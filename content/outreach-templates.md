## Outreach Best Practices

### DO:
- Personalize every message (reference their content, follower count, niche)
- Lead with the money they're losing — it's the strongest hook
- Mention the Founder Program exclusivity (scarcity drives action)
- Position as "try alongside OF" not "leave OF"
- Be genuinely helpful and human
- Respect "no" — never follow up more than twice
- Track responses in the CRM

### DON'T:
- Mass-blast identical messages
- Send more than 20-30 outreach messages per day per platform
- Use fake urgency ("LAST CHANCE!!!")
- Trash-talk OnlyFans (creators have loyalty to it)
- Lie about features that don't exist yet
- DM creators who have "no DMs" or "no business inquiries" in bio
- Comment on NSFW content directly — keep it professional

### Timing:
- **Twitter DMs:** Weekday afternoons (2-6 PM EST)
- **Instagram DMs:** Weekday evenings (5-9 PM EST)
- **Emails:** Tuesday-Thursday mornings (9-11 AM EST)
- **Reddit:** Post when threads are active and recent (< 24 hours old)

### Tracking:
- Log every outreach attempt in the CRM
- Track: name, platform, date sent, template used, response status
- Follow up once after 3-5 days if no response
- Mark as "not interested" after 2 unanswered messages
- Move warm leads to a separate pipeline for onboarding
