### Why SAFE (Not Priced Equity)

- Industry standard for pre-seed / early seed
- No board seats or complex governance at this stage
- Fast close — no lengthy legal negotiations
- Converts to equity at the next priced round (Series Seed or A)
- Investor-friendly: valuation cap + discount protects early investors

## 5. Competitive Advantage

### Why Creators Switch

| Feature | OnlyFans | OpenFans |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Creator payout | 80% | **90-95%** |
| Payout speed | 7-21 days | **Instant (Solana)** |
| Payout method | Bank transfer (limited countries) | **USDC to any wallet (global)** |
| Transaction fees | High (card processing) | **<$0.01 (Solana)** |
| Chargebacks | Creator eats the loss | **None (crypto is final)** |
| Affiliate program | None | **1% lifetime on referrals** |
| Deplatforming risk | High (bank/processor pressure) | **Low (crypto-native)** |

A creator earning **$10,000/month** on OnlyFans keeps $8,000.
On OpenFans they keep **$9,500**.

That's **$18,000 more per year** — per creator.

For a top creator earning $100K/month, the difference is **$180,000/year**.

## 7. Risk Factors & Mitigations

| Risk | How We Address It |
|------|-------------------|
| **Cold-start (no creators or fans)** | Founder Program with exclusive incentives; creator-to-creator affiliate referrals; direct outreach to mid-tier OF creators ($1K-$10K/mo) who benefit most from fee savings |
| **5% take rate may not sustain operations** | Volume thesis — at $100M GMV, 5% = $5M net revenue. Also, 10% on adult content provides higher margin on majority of traffic |
| **Payment processor / banking risk** | Crypto-native from day one; USDC on Solana eliminates traditional processor dependency |
| **Content moderation liability** | Budget allocated for legal counsel + moderation tooling; clear ToS and creator guidelines from launch |
| **OnlyFans retaliates on pricing** | OF is structurally unable to cut to 5% — their $684M profit and $497M dividends depend on 20%. We are the low-cost disruptor |
| **Solo founder risk** | First hire is growth/community lead; advisory board in formation |
| **Regulatory uncertainty (crypto)** | USDC is the most regulated stablecoin; Solana is US-based; compliance-first approach |
